Richard A. Drews is a lawyer in Omaha, Nebraska with over 30 years of experience in estate planning, probate and estate administration, and civil litigation and trial practice throughout the courts of the State of Nebraska. Rick’s philosophy is to provide straight-forward, practical advice on a cost-effective basis to address the legal issues of his clients and achieve their goals.

Rick has received the AV rating from Martindale-Hubbell, which is the highest legal and ethical rating from his peers. Many of Rick’s clients come from referrals from other lawyers, but mostly by word of mouth from his existing clients. He has developed long-term relationships with many of his clients, who have come to trust Rick’s knowledge, experience and counsel for answers to their legal questions and solutions to their legal issues.

Rick has also acted as local counsel for out-of-state lawyers and firms that are in need of representation in Nebraska from someone who can provide experienced local knowledge and excellent service on a cost-effective basis.Rick and his wife Molly have three grown daughters and two grandchildren. Rick is active in the St. Elizabeth Ann Seton parish, and enjoys crossword puzzles, attending baseball games, and walking his dog.
